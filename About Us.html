About Bestea

Bestea was created for everyone who loves to drink milk tea. In fact, one of the goals of Bestea is to gather more milk tea lovers. Another one of our goals is to encourage non-milk tea lovers to give milk tea a try. In which some of our projects includes giving free milk tea to non-milk tea lovers, and drinking milk tea with everyone in Bestea.
